Lost's Face Match Sytem

The target audience of our project is everyone as we are trying to solve a worldwide problem. Using pictures of the missing, computer vision algorithms attempt to help in finding that missing person. The algorithm loops through the pictures in the database trying to find a match. This will not only benefit missing family members and the homeless, but also residents who are bothered by street beggars.
